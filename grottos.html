Grottes de Mara'a and biking up and down the West Coast

Today we did 31kms on bikes, which is pretty good considering we spent most of this year hardly getting out of our apartment in Warsaw. We definitely couldn't do that 2 weeks ago. We went from our base in Puna'Auia at PK13 to Grottes de Mara'a at PK28.5. They are stunning, jungle covered caverns in the side of the cliff just by the road.

On the way back we stopped at a beautiful, practically empty white sand beach.
